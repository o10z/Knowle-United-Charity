Examples of Previous Grants

Knowle United Charity has provided benefits to local residents for over 300 years under the individual Trusts and the amalgamated trusts. In recent years, grants have been made towards:

Local Churches

Grants to support youth workers. 

Organisations

Church halls, community halls and other organisations to assist with equipment in catering facilities and equipment for young or vulnerable group activities.

Schools

Most years grants are made to several schools to assist in the provision of facilities for the chidren, including books, educational and sports equipment, a new play area,a trim track, and a peace garden.

During Covid

Grants totalling £120,000 over two years were made to local schools during Covid restrictions to support additional needs of children.

Education Grants

Education Grants to students towards their further education costs.

Travel Grants

Travel grants to local young people to participate in Charitable local community projects or research projects abroad and locally

Scouts/Sports Club

Scouts groups and Sports Club replacement equipment.

Sheltered accommodation

specific items for renovations in Berrow Homes.

Individuals

Grants to individuals in significant financial need for a particular purpose.
