Guidance Notes
  • Please attach evidence of your University / College place / Student status with your application.
  • Applications for Education Grants should be submitted prior to 15th October and will be considered by the Trustees at their November Meeting. Grants are not automatically given year on year and you must apply for a grant in each year of study.
  • You must normally be under 21 years old at the date of the first application, although older applicants will be considered. A Post Graduate application will be considered but the total of all education grants made will be limited to THREE.
  • You must be resident in the Charity’s area of benefit (i.e. the Parishes of Knowle, Dorridge, Bentley Heath, Barston and the Civil Parishes of Balsall Common and Chadwick End.)
  • For security purposes all correspondence will be directed to your home address and not to University/College Halls of Residence or to your accommodation in the area of study
  • If you are under under 18 years, this application must be also be signed by your parent or legal guardian to consent to the application being made and payment of a grant to you.
  • The Decision of the Trustees as to the award of a Grant is final, and no correspondence can be entered into.
  • Any money awarded and not used for the specified purpose should be returned to the Clerk to the Trustees.

All information disclosed to us will be treated in the strictest confidence.
