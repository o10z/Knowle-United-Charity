Governance and Management

  • Knowle United Charity is managed by 12 Trustees and their Clerk, Simon Chaloner, who carries out the day to day business and management of the properties. The activities of the Charity are determined by the Governing document, the Constitution, and the Trustees have a duty to ensure that the Charity functions in accordance with the Constitution.
  • The Trustees meetings are held in March, July and November each year and members of the Property Sub-Committee and the Clerkâ€™s Sub-Committee also meet whenever necessary.
  • The Charity owns the land on which Knowle Church of England Primary Academy junior school is built and has for many years made grants to the school under the former Knowle Educational Charity.
  • From the income the Charity receives from its properties and investments, the Trustees make various grants to local residents and groups for the relief of need and for the promotion of education in the area. The Charity annual accounts and reports are available on the Charity Commission website.
