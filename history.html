History of Knowle United Charity

Knowle United Charity is made up of a number of small trusts and bequests set up by benevolent local donors over the last 300 years. The Charity traces its origins back to 1592 when the first Trust was set up and this and other small Trusts have been amalgamated over the years since.

History of Knowle United Charity

  • Fourteen of these Trusts were amalgamated in 1992 and became Knowle United Charities.
  • Similarly in 1992 the Harborne Trusts were amalgamated with the Knowle Parochial Charity and became the Knowle Educational Charity in which Knowle Primary School was a named beneficiary.
  • In 2016 Knowle United Charities and Knowle Educational Charity were amalgamated to become to become a Charitable Incorporated Organisation and renamed Knowle United Charity (KUC).
