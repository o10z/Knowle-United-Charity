Grant Applications

The Charity’s Trustees are able to consider applications for grants for residents in the ecclesiastical parishes of Knowle, Dorridge, Barston and Temple Balsall and the civil parishes of Balsall Common and Chadwick End. Grants may be awarded at the discretion of the Trustees for the relief of need and to promote educational purposes.

1. General Grants

Relief of Need: Applications for a grant will be considered by the Trustees from eligible individuals who may be suffering exceptional need or hardship and grants may be given to assist with a specific need. If you, or some-one you know, has a significant financial need, complete an application form and send it to our Clerk.

Relief of need and promotion of education: Grants may also be given to individuals or organisations which provide benefit or support to vulnerable groups of people who reside within the Charity’s specified areas. During the recent Covid restrictions significant grants totalling £120.000 over two years were made to schools within our prescribed areas to support additional needs of children.

Groups which the Charity has supported include equipment for local Church youth groups, playgroups, children’s play areas, scouts and sheltered accommodation improvements and many more. The Charity prefers applications for a specific item or part of a project. Supporting documentation will be required with the application form which should be sent to the Clerk.
General Grant Application Form

2. Educational Grants to Students

Applications for a non means tested grant towards the costs of further eduction will be considered by the Trustees from school leavers aged 18 years and over whose home is within the areas specified in the Charity’s governing document. Educational grants are currently in the region of £400 per year and are given to assist students towards the costs of books, computer equipment, travel and costs of accommodation in their further education studies at University or College. Supporting documentation will be required with the application form which should be sent to the Clerk.
Educational Grant Application Form

3. Student Travel Projects

Applications may also be considered from eligible students for a grant towards a travel project which is undertaken for a charitable purpose either in Britain or abroad. Supporting documentation will be required with the application form which should be sent to the Clerk.
